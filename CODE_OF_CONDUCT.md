# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Restore-Base to be a practical, respectful place for people building Discord tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent while still being precise.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.
- Do not post private credentials, customer data, or harassment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who make the project unsafe or unproductive.
